1. How long is each appointment?

Maximum 60 minutes.

2. What can I do during my appointment?

- Check the status of the product you want to buy through photographs and videos that our owl will send you once you are in the place.

- Corroborate the information of the provider we will visit.

3. How many places can my owl visit during the appointment?

One place per appointment, that is, one provider per appointment

More than one provider may be contacted by appointment only if they are nearby, within a radius of less than 300 meters. This additional visit is subject to the availability of the time that our Owl needs to move and the time it reaches to correctly perform its functions at the additional point.

4. What data is needed to book an appointment?

DEL CLIENTE DEL PROVEDOR
Full name Company name or Contact person
Contact number Contact number
DNI DNI , Full address (district, street, number and reference)
Gmail (Optional) It is recommended to send the location in the current time

5. How far in advance should I book my appointment?

24 hours in advance is advised.

In urgent cases, contact support to try to make an immediate appointment (subject to the availability of collaborators and additional costs).

6. How do I communicate with my owl?

Once the appointment is made, you will receive a message via WhatsApp from one of our collaborators to coordinate details such as location, references or perhaps some recommendations that you should take into account when approaching the appointment.

Please note that only one number can be registered, that is, our Owl will only have contact with one client during the appointment.

7. How can I pay?

Currently we work with the payment method via YAPE or through bank transfers to a BCP account, the data to make this payment will be sent to the client once they have sent us the necessary information to book an appointment and set a specific time.

Remember that you must make the payment within a period of no more than 2 hours after booking your appointment, otherwise the time you reserved will be available and can be taken by another client.

8. Do you offer additional services?

In case of additionally needing the transfer service of the product to some point within the city of Lima, this will have an additional cost. In case the shipment is to the province, this additional cost will only cover transportation within the city, that is, it does not include shipping costs or product or merchandise packaging.

In both cases, this additional service must be previously coordinated with support at the time of booking the appointment.

9. VERY IMPORTANT

- We do not reimburse money for postponement of the appointment or change of plans, so it is very important that you contact your provider before scheduling the appointment.

- If you want to change the date or time of the appointment, you must communicate a support at least 5 hours in advance of the previously agreed appointment and will have a penalty of 15% additional to the cost. This amount must be paid before reserving a new date and time.
